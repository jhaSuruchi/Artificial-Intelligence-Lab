How to Run

Command format:

python3.x assg02.py <input-file> <mode> <parameters>

Modes:

Part 1:
python3 assg02.py input.txt part1 <N> <K>

Part 2:
python3 assg02.py input.txt part2 <N> <days>

Part 3(a):
python3 assg02.py input.txt part3a <N> <K>

Part 3(b):
python3 assg02.py input.txt part3b <N> <days>
